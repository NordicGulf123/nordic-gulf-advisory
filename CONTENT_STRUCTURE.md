## Content Tone & Voice

**Overall Tone:** Professional, trustworthy, data-driven, approachable
**Key Principles:**
- Emphasize 13+ years direct developer experience
- Avoid risky claims ("guaranteed," "prequalified")
- Focus on authentic, experience-backed advice
- Use data and statistics to support claims
- Address investor concerns transparently
- Highlight Scandinavian transparency and professionalism
- Be honest about market risks and considerations

**Language:**
- Clear, readable English
- Avoid jargon unless explained
- Use active voice
- Short paragraphs and sentences
- Scannable format with headers and lists
